Description

We are creating a self sufficient indoor hydroponics system which anyone can install at their home and can grow vegetables (even off season) inside their house without sunlight and without any prior knowledge of farming with help of our fully automated product. All the parameters such as PH, TDS/EC, Lights, Temperature, Humidity will be monitored and controlled using IOT. Since most of the hydroponics technology in India is mainly focused on commercial growers, we are trying to create a unique product for home growers where they won't need to bother anything all their plants will be taken care of through our Automated System.
